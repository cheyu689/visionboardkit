Vision Board Checklist

Complete your vision board in 30 minutes with this step-by-step guide

1Choose Your Theme and Format 2 min

Select a theme that resonates with your goals (Career, Money, Health, Relationship, Study, or Travel) and choose between printable or digital format.

2Select or Create a Template 3 min

Browse our template collection and pick one that matches your style, or create your own custom layout.

3Gather Your Images and Quotes 10 min

Collect photos, magazine cutouts, printed quotes, and visual elements that inspire you. Consider:

  • Personal photos that represent your goals
  • Inspirational quotes and affirmations
  • Images from magazines or printed from online sources
  • Symbolic objects or places

4Arrange and Paste Your Vision Elements 10 min

Lay out your selected template and start arranging your images and quotes. Trust your intuition and place items where they feel right. Consider:

  • Balance and visual flow
  • Grouping related items together
  • Leaving space for affirmations and goals
  • Making sure everything fits without overcrowding

5Add Final Touches and Affirmations 5 min

Add empowering words, affirmations, or decorative elements. Display your vision board where you'll see it daily.
