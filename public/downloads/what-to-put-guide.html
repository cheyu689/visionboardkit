What to Put on Your Vision Board

A complete guide by theme to help you create a powerful and meaningful vision board

Career Professional Goals

Focus on your career aspirations, professional growth, and work-related achievements.

What to Include:

  • Images: Your dream office, professional workspace, industry leaders you admire
  • Words: Promotion, Leadership, Entrepreneurship, Remote Work, Networking
  • Goals: Target job title, salary goal, certification to earn, business to start
  • Symbols: Briefcase, graduation cap, award ribbon, office building
Example: Photo of your dream workspace + word "EXECUTIVE" + image of target company logo + quote "I am a respected leader in my field"

Money Financial Freedom

Visualize your financial goals, abundance mindset, and wealth-building strategies.

What to Include:

  • Images: Dream house, travel destinations, investment portfolio growth charts
  • Words: Financial Freedom, Abundance, Debt Free, Passive Income, Savings Goal
  • Numbers: Specific savings targets, debt payoff amounts, income goals
  • Symbols: Coins, growing plants, upward arrows, piggy bank, safe
Example: Image of dream house + "$100K Savings" + word "ABUNDANCE" + photo of peaceful retirement scene

Health Wellness & Fitness

Create a vision for physical health, mental wellbeing, and self-care practices.

What to Include:

  • Images: Fit body inspiration, healthy meals, yoga poses, peaceful nature scenes
  • Words: Energy, Strength, Balance, Self-Care, Mental Health, Vitality
  • Goals: Target weight, exercise frequency, healthy habits to build
  • Symbols: Heart, running shoes, meditation cushion, water bottle, gym equipment
Example: Photo of active lifestyle + "WORKOUT 5x/week" + word "VITALITY" + healthy meal images

Relationship Love & Connection

Focus on romantic relationships, family bonds, friendships, and self-love.

What to Include:

  • Images: Happy couples, family gatherings, friends having fun, self-care moments
  • Words: Love, Partnership, Communication, Trust, Self-Love, Connection, Boundaries
  • Goals: Quality time commitments, relationship improvements, self-worth affirmations
  • Symbols: Hearts, intertwined circles, family photos, friendship symbols
Example: Family photo + "QUALITY TIME" + self-love affirmation + romantic destination images

Study Learning & Growth

Visualize educational achievements, skill development, and intellectual growth.

What to Include:

  • Images: Graduation ceremony, library scenes, books, certificates, learning spaces
  • Words: Learning, Growth Mindset, Knowledge, Focus, Discipline, Achievement
  • Goals: Degrees to earn, skills to learn, books to read, courses to complete
  • Symbols: Books, graduation cap, lightbulb, pencils, graduation scroll
Example: Graduation cap + "MASTER'S DEGREE" + stack of books + "GROWTH MINDSET" quote

Travel Adventure & Exploration

Manifest your travel dreams and adventure goals.

What to Include:

  • Images: Dream destinations, landmarks, beaches, mountains, cities you want to visit
  • Words: Adventure, Explore, Wanderlust, Freedom, Culture, Experience
  • Goals: Specific destinations, travel dates, budget for trips, experiences to have
  • Symbols: Airplane, passport, globe, compass, suitcase, map markers
Example: Santorini sunset + "JAPAN 2025" + airplane icon + "ADVENTURE AWAITS" quote

  • Keep it focused: Choose 6-10 meaningful goals rather than overwhelming yourself
  • Use positive language: Frame goals as "I am" rather than "I want to be"
  • Make it personal: Include photos that mean something to you personally
  • Balance visuals and words: A mix creates a stronger emotional impact
  • Review regularly: Place your vision board somewhere you'll see it daily
